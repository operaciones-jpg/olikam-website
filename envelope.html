Premium Architectural Envelopes,
Turnkey

Design, fabrication and installation of metal facades, metal roofs and luxury windows & doors. Certified quality and a validated global manufacturing network — no owned plant, full flexibility.

OLIKAM ENVELOPE is our core unit: high-performance building envelopes engineered and delivered end-to-end. We integrate parametric design, certified fabrication through a multi-country network, and precision installation under a single point of contact — from early design-assist to final commissioning.

What We Build

Products

Metal Facades

Complex parametric and geometric facades, perforated aluminum panels, louvers and dynamic modules. Ventilated systems (BEMO, Germany / STENI, Norway).

Metal Roofs

Curved and 3D standing seam with PVDF coatings. Airports, stadiums, industrial buildings. BEMO Systems technology.

Luxury Windows & Doors

GOYA + DALÍ systems. Polyamide thermal break, VITRO Solarban / Guardian SNX glass. Steel, aluminum and UPVC with double or triple IGU.
