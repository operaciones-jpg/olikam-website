Our Business Units

OLIKAM operates as a specialized holding with four complementary units covering the entire facade lifecycle.

OLIKAM RENOVA

MX

Keep your facades flawless without the need for replacement. We offer cleaning, maintenance, and deep sealing services, executed by a highly specialized team for high-altitude work. Additionally, we renovate the building's image using high-durability architectural adhesive films.

MX + USA

3D LiDAR precision diagnosis before fabrication. Eliminates geometric errors, reduces on-site rework, and generates the as-built model (Revit/IFC) that feeds engineering and fabrication directly. ±2mm precision.

Products We Offer

Premium custom-engineered architectural products

Metal Facades

Complex parametric and geometric facades, perforated aluminum panels, louvers and dynamic modules. Ventilated systems using the best materials and finishes.

Metal Roofs

Curved and 3D standing seam of high complexity with durable PVDF coatings. Large surfaces: airports, stadiums and industrial buildings.

Luxury Windows & Doors

Luxury windows with the highest thermal performance, minimalist profiles and maximum openings. Thermal break in steel, aluminum and UPVC with double or triple IGU and Low-E glass.

Facade Maintenance & Refurbishment

Periodic specialized cleaning, remedial sealing, and advanced facade modernization featuring architectural membranes.
